The Sanctuary Story

Chapter 1: The Place

Nestled deep within 30 acres of protected jungle, our sanctuary offers a landscape untouched by commercial tourism. Here, towering bamboo groves provide natural shade, and flowing streams offer cool respite from the tropical heat.

We have removed the fences and cages, allowing the herd to forage, roam, and socialize exactly as they would in the wild. The terrain is varied and vast, challenging their minds and bodies in the healthiest way possible. This is not just a location; it is a recovered ecosystem returned to its rightful owners.

Our Core Values

Chapter 2: The Principles

True sanctuary means placing the needs of the elephant above the desires of the human. We operate on a strict "hands-off" policy for the majority of the day, ensuring that our elephants are never forced to perform, ride, or behave unnaturally for entertainment.

Dignity is our guiding star. We believe that every elephant deserves a retirement free from chains and hooks. Our role is merely that of a guardianâ€”providing medical care, nutrition, and love, while stepping back to let nature take its course.

SUPPORT & PREPARATION

Common Questions

What should I wear and bring?

keyboard_arrow_down

We recommend wearing comfortable, light clothing that you don't mind getting a little dirty. Closed-toe shoes or sturdy sandals are best for walking in the sanctuary. Don't forget sunscreen, insect repellent, a hat, and a reusable water bottle. If you're joining the mud spa or bathing activities, please bring a change of clothes and swimwear.

Is this suitable for children and the elderly?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, absolutely! Our sanctuary is designed to be accessible for visitors of all ages. The walking paths are generally flat and easy to navigate. We welcome families and have had guests from toddlers to grandparents enjoy their time with the elephants. However, please let us know in advance if you have any mobility concerns so we can assist you.

What is the cancellation policy?

keyboard_arrow_down

We understand that plans can change. Cancellations made at least 24 hours before your scheduled visit are fully refundable. Cancellations made within 24 hours are non-refundable, but we are happy to help you reschedule your visit for a future date, subject to availability.

Are transfers included from my hotel?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, complimentary round-trip transfers are included for most hotels in the main areas of Phuket (Patong, Kata, Karon, Kamala). For hotels in more remote areas, a small surcharge may apply. Please provide your hotel details when booking, and we will confirm your pick-up time.

How do we ensure the elephants' wellbeing?

keyboard_arrow_down

Our elephants' wellbeing is our top priority. We adhere to strict ethical guidelines: no riding, no chains, and no hooks. Our "hands-off" observation model allows elephants to socialize and forage naturally. We have a team of dedicated veterinarians and mahouts who provide round-the-clock care, ensuring they are healthy, happy, and free.
